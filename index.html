BELIEVE.

This is a story about one of the greatest comeback in sports history.

On 27 December 1964, the Cleveland Browns defeated the Baltimore Colts to win the National Football League Championship.

From that day on, the city of Cleveland would not see another major sports championship

for over 50 years.

“With the 1st pick in the 2003 NBA draft, the Cleveland Cavaliers select... Lebron James!”

The NBA draft.

A day where aspiring basketball players look forward to getting selected by professional teams.

For the 18-year-old kid from Akron, the jump to the professional elite came amazingly fast.

There was excitement surrounding what the Cavaliers can accomplish. The future looked extremely bright.

However, Lebron's championship journey was riddled with many failures.

In 2007, he brought his team to the NBA finals for the 1st time. But they lost to the Spurs, who were veterans.

With those words, Lebron decided to join his friends on the Miami Heat, receiving tons of hate and criticisms.
